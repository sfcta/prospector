Bicycle and Pedestrian Collision Data

This data is the observed collisions in San Francisco thanks to the SWITRS database.
  • Currently, the points are the exact location in which the collision occurred.
  • Only collisions under the San Francisco area.
  • Data is complete for years 2006-2014.
  • Data is not entirely complete according to SWITRS information for years 2015-2016.
